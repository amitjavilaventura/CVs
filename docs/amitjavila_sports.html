One of the most relevant components in my life has been sport, specially rowing, and here I will show some the results I’ve achieved.

Rowing

I started rowing at the end of 2007 and, since then, I have trained and competed at my highest level to achieve my goals in this sport.

Unfortunatelly, I missed the qualification for the 2016 Olympic Games by one place. However, toghether with my crewmate, I became 2016 World Rowing Under-23 champion in the category of Under-23 Lightweight Double Scull (BLM2x).

I left the high-level competition in 2019 and, since 2022 I am involved in the organization of rowing competititons in Banyoles.
